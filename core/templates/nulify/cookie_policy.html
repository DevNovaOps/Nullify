Cookie Policy

How Nullify uses cookies and similar technologies. Last updated: March 2026.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They allow websites to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you interact with the service. Similar technologies such as session storage and local storage may be used in an equivalent manner.

2. How We Use Cookies

Nullify uses cookies solely for the following essential purposes:

  • Session Management: We use server-side Django session cookies to keep you authenticated while you navigate the platform. Without this cookie, you would be logged out every time you navigate to a new page.
  • CSRF Protection: We use a CSRF (Cross-Site Request Forgery) token cookie on all forms to protect your account from cross-site attacks.
  • Theme Preference: We store your selected colour theme (dark/light/system) in your browser's local storage so the platform remembers your preference between visits.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Session and CSRF cookies — these are essential for the platform to function and cannot be disabled.
  • Preference Cookies: Theme selection stored in local storage — these enhance your experience but are not required for core functionality.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Nullify does not embed any third-party services (such as social media plugins, advertising networks, or analytics platforms) that would set third-party cookies on your device. All processing happens locally on our server.

5. Cookie Duration

  • Session Cookie: Expires when you close your browser or log out.
  • CSRF Token: Expires at the end of the browser session.
  • Theme Preference (localStorage): Persists indefinitely until you clear your browser data or change the setting.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies (session and CSRF) will prevent you from logging in and using the platform. For guidance on managing cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the platform or applicable regulations. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at nullifyorg@gmail.com.
