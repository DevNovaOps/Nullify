About Us

Building a Safer Digital World

Nullify is a rule-based PII detection and sanitization platform built to help organizations protect sensitive data without relying on AI models.

Our Mission

Privacy-First Data Protection

We believe every organization should have powerful tools to detect and remove personally identifiable information from their data. No cloud dependencies. No AI black boxes. Just transparent, regex-powered PII detection.

Security First

All processing happens locally. Your data never leaves your server — no cloud APIs, no third-party services.

Transparent Rules

Every detection is powered by well-defined regex patterns. No opaque ML models — you know exactly what's being detected.

Role-Based Access

Granular role-based access control ensures only authorized personnel can access sensitive data and admin functions.
