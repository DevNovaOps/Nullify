6. What Is PII? 
Personally Identifiable Information (PII) refers to any data that can be used to identify or trace an individual’s identity, 
either directly or when combined with other information.

Note: Non -Personally Identifiable Information (Non -PII) refers to data that cannot be used to identify an individual directly or indir ectly. It does 
not contain names, contact details, or any relational information that could link the data to a specific person.
